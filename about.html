Our Values

01

Gracious Professionalism

We compete hard and help others harder. FIRST's core principle is woven into everything we do.

02

Iterate Relentlessly

Our first idea is never our best. We prototype, test, break, and rebuild until it works.

03

Inclusive by Design

Every voice on the team matters. Diverse perspectives build better robots and better people.

04

Community First

We exist to serve our community, not just our scoreboard. Outreach is as important as competition.
